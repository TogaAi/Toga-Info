About TogaAI

TogaAI is a cutting-edge Shopify app that empowers fashion brands, retailers, and entrepreneurs to showcase their products like never before. Using advanced AI-powered virtual try-on technology, we allow users to visualize how their clothing items would look on different models — or even on themselves — without the need for expensive photoshoots.

What You Can Do Today

  • Upload your own clothing images or select existing ones from your Shopify product catalog
  • Choose from a diverse selection of realistic AI-generated models
  • See how your items look on different models instantly
  • Enable Pro Mode for improved image quality and text/logo clarity
  • Use Upscaling to boost image resolution
  • Generate up to 8 unique poses per item for dynamic presentation
